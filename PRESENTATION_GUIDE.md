## üéì TIPS FOR SUCCESS

### Before Meeting
1. **Know your numbers** - Practice ROI calculation
2. **Rehearse opening** - First 2 minutes are critical
3. **Prepare examples** - Have 2-3 real job failure examples ready
4. **Anticipate concerns** - Review Q&A section
5. **Dress professionally** - Make good first impression

### During Meeting
1. **Start strong** - Lead with the ask & ROI
2. **Use visuals** - Point to diagrams, not walls of text
3. **Listen actively** - Let architects express concerns
4. **Answer precisely** - Use specific numbers, not vague estimates
5. **Stay calm** - Address skepticism with data, not emotion
6. **End clear** - Confirm next meeting, clear action items

### After Meeting
1. **Follow up** - Email within 24 hours
2. **Address concerns** - Respond to any raised questions
3. **Confirm timeline** - Next meeting to define success metrics
4. **Assign ownership** - Who's driving Phase 1?
5. **Keep momentum** - Schedule kickoff meeting
